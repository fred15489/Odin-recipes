    • Description

    This is a delicious Bruschetta with mozzarella recipe that will impress your friends and family.

    Ingredients

    • balsamic
    • vinegar
    • cherry

    Steps

    1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Slice the baguette or Italian bread into 1/2-inch thick slices.
    3. Place the bread slices on a baking sheet and drizzle them with olive oil.
    4. Toast the bread slices in the preheated oven for about 10 minutes, or until they turn golden and crispy.
    5. While the bread is toasting, prepare the topping. Dice the tomatoes into small pieces and place them in a bowl.
    6. Finely chop the fresh basil leaves and add them to the bowl with the tomatoes.
    7. Mince the garlic cloves and add them to the bowl as well.
    8. Drizzle the tomato mixture with olive oil and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Mix well to combine.
    9. Take the toasted bread slices out of the oven and let them cool slightly.
    10. Slice the fresh mozzarella cheese into thin slices.
    11. Place a slice of mozzarella on each toasted bread slice.
    12. Spoon the tomato mixture on top of the mozzarella slices, distributing it evenly.
    13. If desired, you can drizzle some additional olive oil and balsamic vinegar on top for extra flavor.
    14. Serve the Bruschetta with Mozzarella immediately as an appetizer or snack.
